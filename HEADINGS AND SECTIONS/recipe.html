Simple Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Directions

  1. preheat your oven to 3500F (1900).
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. place spoonfull of dough on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.

Cooking Terms

Preheat
Heat (an oven or grill) beforehand.
Bake
Cook (food) by dry heat without directly exposing to a flame, typically in an oven.
